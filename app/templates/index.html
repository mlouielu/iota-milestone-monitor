IOTA is a DAG-based cryptocurrency, it's consensus algorithm is based on IOTA Whitepaper, by each new transaction to select two new tips to validate, and causing tangle (the IOTA network) to changed previous transaction's weight. After the transaction's wegiht over a threshold, it will be taken as a valid transaction.

Currently, in the tangle network, a transaction's status (confirmed or not) is controled by a special address called "Coordinator", this "Coordinator" is now controled by IOTA Foundation. In a period time, Coordinator will issue a new transactioni called "milestone" that indicated IOTA nodes to changed tangle's states. When a transaction is direct or indirect pointed by milestone, it will be treated as a "Confirmed" transaction.

Coordinator is to protect beta time tangle, to prevent malicious attack when the tangle isn't strong enough to prevent attack. And will be removed in someday.
